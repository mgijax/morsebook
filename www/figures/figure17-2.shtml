FIGURE 2. Electrophoretic forms of glutamate oxaloacetate transaminases ( 16). Electrophoretic variants of the mitochondrial isozyme are illustrated. These are apparent when enzymes from inbred strains SWR/J and C57BL/6J are compared. The mutation affecting the electrophoretic mobility of GOT-2 does not affect GOT-1, so the mitochondrial and soluble forms of GOT are structurally different proteins controlled by different genetic loci.
