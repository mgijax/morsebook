FIGURE 5. Isozymes of glucose phosphate isomerase found in embryos of various ages ( 10). The mother is Gpi-1a/Gpi-1a, the father is Gpi-1b/Gpi-1b, the embryo Gpi-1a/Gpi-1b. Phenotype GPI-1A is present in the mother, GPI-1B in the father. GPI-1AB is the pattern expected in a heterozygous adult. Blastocysts were removed from the mother on days 3, 4, 5, and 6 after fertilization and their isozymic patterns were examined by starch gel electrophoresis. GPI-1AB and GPI-1B which represent paternal gene products were first observed on day 5.
