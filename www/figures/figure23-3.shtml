FIGURE 3. Tryptic peptide maps of MMTV gp52s. Two-dimensional fingerprints of the gp52s from the MMTVs were performed as described ( 16, 17). The peptide maps of gp52s from mouse and feline grown viruses are shown in the top and bottom rows, respectively. The arrows in the MMTV(C3H) gp52 maps show an additional peptide which distinguishes this protein from the gp52s of the RIII and GR viruses.
